Part 2

500 Years ahead since the tragedy

A teenager living in the fortress made by the villagemen in the empire,after countless years of battle with the rebellion which spread through the entire kingdom and the empire,The empire had proven itself worthy against the attacks,the resistance always lasted,and there lived in the kingdom a teen with great ambition nor he lived in luxury nor was he royal blood but yet the townsfolk had great respect for him,an elegant gentleman he was,the fights with the rebellion had been decreasing,the last fight being taken place 3 years ago where the gentleman treated the wounds of the villagers.This gentleman's name was Zephyrin,but he was also known as Zorren,meaning The Light.

The empire and the rebellious group had made significant and remarkable changes in their advancement and technology in these 500 years. The Dreadborne(rebellious group)was now known as the Crimson-blaze and the members of the rebellious group now known as Duskborne as the name suggests they rampaged and scattered in the battlefield and pulled deadly maneuvers during dusk.

Hence same applied for the villagemen and the entire empire except a few parts, they were now known as Eldermere and the villagers and folks living in the empire being subdivided into two categories The elder-Men were the nobles and incharge,as their name suggests they had immense knowledge and made strong and bold decisions. The passants,knights and civilians were the Orrenfolks

The people of the Eldermere shall enjoy harmony while it lasts for thy nor thou know the terrors which shall lie on the eyes soon Shall or shall not the Eldermere deflect these terrors? Shall be known as time passes
